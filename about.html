About The E-Waste Project

Creating a Cleaner and Greener Future

Who We Are

E-Waste Collection System is an environmentally friendly platform designed to manage and recycle electronic waste safely. Our mission is to reduce pollution caused by old electronic devices and promote sustainable recycling practices.


We provide easy pickup services for electronic waste including computers, laptops, mobile phones, batteries, televisions, printers, and other electronic devices.

Our Mission

To build a cleaner environment by collecting and recycling electronic waste responsibly.

Our Vision

To create awareness about e-waste management and encourage eco-friendly recycling habits.

Our Goal

To reduce harmful environmental effects caused by improper disposal of electronic products.

Items After Collection

We recognize how greatly our planet benefits from proper E-Waste processing and recycling. Upon collection of any item we make sure that all materials can be recovered, reused or refurbished to the maximum extent. While materials that can't be reused are disposed of in a proper manner, eliminating the risk of hazardous toxins and chemicals from escaping into the Earth.

Quality Ensured

We use multiple recycling plants that are fully certified and accredited by The ANSI-ASQ National Accreditation Board and up to EPA standards. Standards include: Responsible Recycling ("R2") and e-Stewards Standard for Responsible Recycling certifications.

Certified Results

Our Certified recyclers demonstrate through audits and other means that they meet high environmental standards and can safely manage e-waste. Continual oversight by certifying bodies assures they are held to those standards.
